A Woman's Place is in the House and the Senate

Examining Female Political Participation in the United States

In 2016, the United States had its first female presidential nominee, Hillary Clinton. While she failed to capture the presidency, she ignited a score of politically active women. In this interactive data viz, we will explore political participation of women in the US House of Representatives, Senate, State Legislative Bodies and Statewide Elected Positions, as well as the percentage of women who voted in the 2016 presidential election. Data comes from Institute for Women's Policy Research
